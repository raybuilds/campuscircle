Crisis & Transition

Use this page when something urgent is happening. Follow the steps calmly. These are practical actions, not advice articles.

Immediate Danger

Use this if you are in physical danger, being threatened, or feel unsafe right now.

Action

Call: 112

What to say

  • Say your exact location
  • Explain what is happening right now
  • Ask for immediate help

What happens next

  • Emergency services are dispatched
  • You may be asked to stay on the line
  • Follow instructions calmly

Ragging or Harassment

Use this if you are facing ragging, intimidation, or mental harassment.

Action

Call: 1800-180-5522
Email: helpline@antiragging.in

What to do now

  • Move to a safe, public place
  • Note names, dates, and locations
  • Contact a trusted adult if possible

What happens next

  • Complaint is officially recorded
  • Authorities are notified
  • Protection steps may be initiated

Sudden Eviction or Forced Move

Use this if your PG/hostel asks you to vacate suddenly or locks you out.

What to do

  • Stay calm and avoid arguments
  • Collect your documents and essentials
  • Contact friends for temporary stay

Immediate steps

  • Document messages or notices
  • Contact local authorities if threatened
  • Use Campus Circle to explore alternatives

Feeling Overwhelmed or Panicked

Use this if anxiety, panic, or emotional distress feels unmanageable.

Action

Call: 14416 (Tele-MANAS)

What to say

  • Describe how you are feeling
  • Say what triggered it
  • Ask for immediate emotional support

What happens next

  • You speak to a trained professional
  • They help stabilize the situation
  • You may receive follow-up guidance
